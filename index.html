About

Code meets
creativity.

I'm a Creative Computing graduate with a focus on the intersection of technology and human experience. My work spans web development, UI/UX design, and interactive game environments — each project an exploration of how digital tools can tell stories.

With three years of hands-on experience in full-stack web development, user interface design, and game development, I approach every project with a maker's mindset — I don't just plan, I build.

I'm driven by curiosity: why does an interaction feel satisfying? How does layout guide attention? What makes a game world feel alive? These questions shape everything I make.

Design & Digital Philosophy

"Good digital work is honest — it solves real problems, respects the user's intelligence, and leaves room for delight. I believe the best interfaces disappear, and the best games feel like a second reality. Technology should always be in service of the human using it."
